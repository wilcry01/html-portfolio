Ryan's Favorite Movies

My Top 3 Miyazaki Films

#1 Spirited Away

This was the first Miyazaki film I ever watched, so there is a lot of sentimental value for me. I was lucky enough to see it in theaters when it released in the U.S.. I was blown away by the visuals and incredible story.

#2 Princess Mononoke

I love dark, complex themes of this film. I think it is an interesting commentary on the power of nature versus industrialization. What a stellar voice cast as well!

#3 Howl's Moving Castle

The whimsy and charm of this movie are spectacular! I loved the exploration on how looks can be deceiving. What a great adaptation of the novel of the same name.
